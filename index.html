About Me

Hi, I'm Graeme

I'm a self-taught designer and programmer specializing in making web applications. I first began studying programming and responsive-design in early 2019 through online courses, documents, and personal projects. Now I work independently with businesses on freelance contracts, but I am also interested in collaborating with others for larger projects. I consider communication a big priority while working with others, so I try to be thorough when asking and answering questions on each job. My workflow includes coding and using design programs to provide software/web-based solutions to people. I mainly do UI/UX design and frontend development, but I'm comfortable working across the full stack. I'm especially experienced in building Javascript applications with React, React-Native, and CSS, but I have also worked on projects using Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and socket.io.
